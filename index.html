Ways to create a more healthy environment for PLMAYERS

Community Problem Concern Solution
Discrimination Towards Irregular Students The majority of irregular students from various colleges feel left out during groupings and groupworks. Be more approachable and open Do not let status (irreg/reg) influence your interactions or serve as the foundation for friendships or relationships between blockmates and peers.
Irregular students feel isolated among their classmates and peers. Be considerate and communicate openly. Express your concerns and listen to the concerns of your peers, particularly in academic matters and schedule/time constraints.
When it comes to academics, the majority of them have no one to rely on other than themselves. Conduct seminars tackling the effects of such actions. The seminar should also include recommendations on how to build relationships on both set of students.
